Release Notes

Game Project: Go Fish
Creator: Gabriel Castaneira
Version: 2.0
Date:  1/15/2024

New features
- The avatar sprite is now directional and will face either Left or Right based on input.
- Implemented a visible Ending sprite to finish the game at the party.

Fixes
- Several dialogue prompts in their spacing were adjusted for proper dialogue flow.

Improvements
- All fish you invite to the party now have random conversational dialogue when interacted.
- All fish at the party now have random conversational dialogue when interacted.

Known Bugs
- The directional flipping mechanic of your avatar reverses directions when being teleported to another room.
